Corporate structure & compliance

Organized, regulated, and contractually bound — by design.

Mohave Technology Training Group operates as a formally registered business entity, not an informal association. Every service we provide is delivered under documented authority, not independent initiative.

§1

Entity formation

Organized as a state-registered LLC with corporate tax election, holding an EIN, applicable business licenses, and jurisdiction-appropriate local permits.

§2

Federal authorization

All flight operations conducted under FAA Part 107, with Remote Pilot Certification maintained for instructional staff and additional waivers, COAs, or BVLOS authorizations obtained per engagement.

§3

Insurance & liability

Commercial general liability and UAS-specific coverage maintained at levels appropriate for public safety and infrastructure-adjacent contract work.

§4

Engagement basis

Services are rendered exclusively under signed contracts or memoranda of understanding with the requesting agency or client — never on independent authority.

§5

Scope exclusions

The company does not provide armed services, independent security enforcement, autonomous tactical operations, or paramilitary training of any kind.

§6

Tax & reporting

Operated transparently as a for-profit, taxable entity, with standard deduction, payroll, and sales-tax treatment applied to all service revenue.

This framework is what distinguishes a commercial UAS services provider from an unaffiliated or unauthorized group: incorporation, licensure, insurance, and a documented client relationship for every operation performed.
